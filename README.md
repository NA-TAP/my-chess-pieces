# my-chess-pieces

A chess piece set containing: an obese knight, 
a smiley faced car elephant, a legless duck (DUCKDUCK), 
a spike trap queen, a forest amazon, two cope lines 
on a king butt, three circles as a pawn, and a dragon. 
Made in Inkscape and MS Paint.

## Pieces included
- Standard pieces (King, Queen, Rook, Bishop, Knight, Pawn)
- Fairy pieces (Archbishop, Chancellor, Amazon)
- Animal family (Camel, Elephant, Giraffe, Zebra)
- Dragon Bishop
- Knightrider
- IMMOBILE
- DUCKDUCK (for Duck Chess — legless, yellow, unbothered)

## Also includes
Baby Chess — a 6x6 variant where the foals 
will always be baby 🐴
